How to Play Forager

Welcome to Forager!

  • Click on a mushroom to go and collect it.
  • Your total distance is shown on the right.
  • To win, collect all the mushrooms without going over the target.
  • Build up a win-streak and get on the leaderboard!
  • Use the settings to adjust the field size and number of mushrooms.
